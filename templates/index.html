Who We Are

A Wholesale Fabric Sourcing hub Built for Import Buyers, Not Just Local Retail

Twil Tex has operated out of Khurrianwala, Faisalabad — Pakistan's largest textile sourcing hub, since 2004, starting as a family-run textile business. Today, led by Founder & CEO Usama Zafar, we coordinate production through a trusted network of partner mills for buyers who need consistent bulk output: hotel groups replacing linen fleets, importers building private label bedding lines, and healthcare distributors sourcing compliant medical textiles.

Every order runs through our own in-line QC checkpoints and a Final Random Inspection (FRI) before it leaves the country, so what you approve in the sample is what arrives in the container.
